History

Established in 2020. The company's history and inspiration was inspired by my daughter Nova, she was a blessing to be born given the conditions her and her mother went through during the Covid pandemic. I had switched jobs several times during the pandemic due to not enough hours or not enough money to survive, I was also layed off one time as well in means to find a career to build a future with the love of my life and our 3 kids (now 4), it seemed like nothing was working so one day I just sat down and thought to asked my self. What can I do in these tuff times? What can I enjoy doing, so it doesn't feel like I'm even working? Then it hit me I started my own business and started small and grew a little more each day little by little, at the same time, our daughter was as well. She learned to talk, she learned to walk and no matter what if she failed, she never gave up. She is my inspiration and drive as well as our family, so I implemented her name in the business IN NOVA TION - (INNOVATION).
